Privacy Policy for Ravens

At Ravens ("we", "our", or "us"), we value your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Data We Collect

We collect and use data solely to enable and improve the functionality of the app.

  • Data from Waarneming.nl: The app uses data from waarneming.nl, which you have authorized through your account. This may include information such as observations and records linked to your account.
  • Personal Data: We do not store login details (such as username or password). These are used only for authentication through Waarneming.nl's secure systems.
  • Non-Personal Data: We may collect technical information such as usage statistics and diagnostic data for troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used to:

  • Deliver and improve the app's functionality.
  • Display your observations through the connection with Waarneming.nl.
  • Resolve issues with the app and provide support.

3. Sharing Data

We do not share your data with third parties, except when:

  • It is necessary for the operation of the app (e.g., through Waarneming.nl).
  • It is required by law.

4. Data Security

We take measures to protect your data:

  • Login credentials for Waarneming.nl are not stored or shared.
  • Communication with Waarneming.nl is conducted via secure, encrypted connections (e.g., HTTPS).

5. Third Parties

The app uses the API of waarneming.nl. Any data you use through the app is processed under Waarneming.nl's privacy policy. Please read their privacy policy for more information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or delete your data through Waarneming.nl.
  • Restrict the use of your data by choosing not to log into the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Please contact us if you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be announced in the app and on this page.
