Privacy Policy for Ravens

At Ravens ("we," "our," or "us"), we highly value your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Data We Collect

We collect and use data solely to enable and improve the app's functionalities.

  • Data from Waarneming.nl: The app uses data from waarneming.nl for which you have granted permission via your account. This may include information such as observations and sightings linked to your account.
  • Personal data: We do not store login credentials (such as username or password). These are only used for logging in via the secure systems of Waarneming.nl.
  • Non-personal data: We may collect technical information such as usage statistics and diagnostic data for troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used to:

  • Deliver and improve the app's functionalities.
  • Display your observations through the connection with Waarneming.nl.
  • Resolve app issues and provide support.

3. Sharing of Data

We do not share your data with third parties unless:

  • It is necessary for the app's functionality (e.g., through Waarneming.nl).
  • It is required by law.

The location data collected by the app is used exclusively for functionalities within the app. This data helps to:

  • Display your current position in relation to observations and sightings.
  • Enhance location-based features, such as finding observations nearby.

We do not store your location data outside the app and do not use it for commercial purposes. By using the app, you explicitly consent to the use of your location data as described above. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device's settings.

4. Data Security

We take measures to protect your data:

  • Login credentials for Waarneming.nl are not stored or shared.
  • Communication with Waarneming.nl occurs via secure, encrypted connections (e.g., HTTPS).

5. Third Parties

The app uses the API of waarneming.nl. The data you use through the app is processed under Waarneming.nl's privacy policy. Read their privacy policy for more information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or delete data via Waarneming.nl.
  • Restrict the use of your data by not logging into the app.

7. Privacy of Children

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be announced in the app and on this page.
